About me

Frontend Developer
Linda Formumm

Located in Berlin, Germany.

After finishing my university degree in computer science with the focus on multimedia and web I worked for some online companies. Additional freelancing projects were implemented for studios in the beauty and health sector. Now I try to make the shift from E-commerce to some more innovative sectors like AI, Web 3.0, VR/AR or biotech.

What is essential in my work?

Performance

Google Pagespeed Optimization, using web dev Tools, build process.

Passion

Intuitive

Dynamic

Websites don't have to be static, I love making pages come to life.

Component-Driven

Creating reusable components with a TDD approach are always my goal.
